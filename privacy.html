Privacy Policy

How We Protect Your Data

We take your privacy seriously and have implemented various security measures to protect your data. These include:

However, we may share your information with trusted partners and service providers who help us operate the website and deliver our services. These partners are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes specified.
