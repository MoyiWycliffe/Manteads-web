WELCOME TO FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS(FAQs).

1.ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT.

How do I create An account?

Click the Sign Up button and fill your details and submit to create your account.

How do i recover my password?

Click the Recover button on the login page,enter your email and follow the instructions.

How do I delete my account?

to delete account,go to account settings,click "Delete account" and follow the prompts.

How do I update my account?

Go to your profile page and click "Update or Edit account details."

Can i change the username,password?

Yes,all changes can be done in your user profile.Login into your profile and Edit changes.Only Email changes cannot be made at this time.

How do I verify my email address?

Email Verification can be done in two ways:On sign up or on your profile.In all ways,You will recieve a Verification code and follow the prompts.

Why can`t I login into my account?

Ensure your Email and password are correct:the registered email and password only are used when loging in.

Can I have multiple accounts?

As for now,you are not allowed to have multiple accounts.

How do I add a profile picture?

Just click on the profile picture section in your profile and follow the steps.

2.Job Listings.

How do I search for a job?

Use the search bar at the top of the home page,enter relevant keywords,job titles,or sort and filter to get the relevant results easily.

Can I filter job listings by locations?

Yes,filters are available by location,job type and others.Choose your best option choice in the filter bar.

How do I apply for a job?

After finding your best job,click on the job to view the details and click apply then follow the procedures.

Can I see the number of job applicants for a job?

We don`t display the number of applicants unless your are the one posted the job due to privacy reasons,but you can see when the job was posted and it is deadline.If the employer added the number of required employees,then you will be able to see it in the job description but not number of applicants.

How do I save a job for later application?

You can save the job and apply later but remember the deadline of the job,also,the employer may have got the required number of applicants or delete it hence you will be left behind even after saving.So,in simple terms,apply job as soon as it is posted or with the deadline is when you can save it.

How do I know the job expired?

Consider the deadline.

Can I apply for multiple jobs?

Yes,But for fremium users,you may have some limitations.Get more information on the premium list.

3.Job Alerts and notifications.

How do I set job alerts?

Go to "notifications" in your account settings,select the criteria and how often you want to recieve the notifications.

How to turn off job alerts?

You can disabled it in your user account settings.

Why am I not recieving job alerts?

Ensure you enabled the job notifications in your account settings and you have the correct email.

4.Application process.

How do I know if my application has been recieved?

You will recieve a confirmation email after applying,and your application status will update soon.

Can I edit my application after submision?

Unfortunately,once submitted,you can`t edit your application.However,you can withdraw it and apply it again.

How do I withdraw my application?

Go to "Applied jobs" in your profile,find job,click "withdraw".

Can I apply for a job without resume?

Some Jobs allow you to apply via a simple application form,but submiting a resume is often prefered.

How long does it take to hear back my application?

Response time vary,but employers typically get back to you within 1-2weeks.
