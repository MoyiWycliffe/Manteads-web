Welcome to MANTEADS Employer Hub

MANTEADS is your trusted digital recruitment platform designed to streamline your hiring process from job posting to candidate selection. Whether you're a startup looking for your first hire or a large enterprise scaling fast, our platform empowers employers with intelligent hiring tools, real-time applicant tracking, and customizable job listings.

With access to thousands of verified job seekers across multiple skill sets and industries, MANTEADS ensures that your listings reach the right talent. Our advanced filtering and job targeting tools boost visibility among relevant candidates, improving hiring efficiency and reducing recruitment costs.

Key Employer Features

  • Post unlimited job vacancies quickly and easily
  • Access a wide pool of job seekers across Kenya and beyond
  • Track applications in real time with our dynamic dashboard
  • Customize your employer profile for better branding and visibility
  • Communicate directly with shortlisted applicants
  • Offer tasks, gigs, or microjobs and manage payouts via our Earn module
  • Promote listings via sponsored placements for higher reach
